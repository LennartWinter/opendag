During the software developer program, you will learn everything about apps and programming. Will you be the professional with knowledge of C#, PHP, and Javascript in the future? You will develop web-based applications, websites, application software, and media, for example. You take into account the customer's requirements and personally test your apps.

A software developer is customer-oriented and builds (parts of) applications or software on behalf of companies, for example. As a software developer, you design in a team and perform tests. You are social, but you can also work well independently. You also consider online security very important.
