During the software developer training, you will learn everything about apps and programming. You will become a professional with knowledge of C#, PHP, and JavaScript. You will be capable of developing web-based applications, websites, application software, and media. Throughout this process, you will always consider the customer's preferences and conduct your own tests. As a software developer, you are highly customer-oriented and work on building applications or software, whether on behalf of companies or not. In this role, you often design as part of a team and perform comprehensive testing procedures. You have a social character but can also work independently. Additionally, you place great importance on online security.
